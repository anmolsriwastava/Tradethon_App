## 🏛 Platform Overview
QuantTrade Sim is a specialized web-based environment designed for quantitative finance enthusiasts to practice price discovery and market-making. Inspired by **IMC Prosperity** and **Optiver Tradethon**, the platform challenges players to identify the "Fair Value" of probabilistic puzzles and trade against sophisticated AI agents.

## 🚀 Core Technologies
*   **Engine:** High-concurrency **Python FastAPI** backend for order matching.
*   **Intelligence:** **Groq AI** integrated puzzle generation (Proprietary Logic).
*   **Real-time:** Sub-millisecond state updates via **WebSockets**.
*   **Infrastructure:** React.js frontend, Supabase (PostgreSQL) persistence, and PWA architecture.

## 📊 Market Microstructure
The platform simulates a realistic trading environment through three distinct liquidity tiers:
*   **Liquidity Providers:** Automated bots maintaining bid-ask spreads.
*   **Informed Agents:** Challenge-tier bots with a probabilistic edge (approx. 70% accuracy).
*   **Noise Participants:** Randomized order flow to simulate market volatility.
